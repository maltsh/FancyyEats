PUMPKIN BREAD
RECENT POST

Ingredients:

1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

1/2 teaspoon ground allspice

1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened

1 1/2 cups granulated sugar

2 large eggs

1 cup canned pumpkin puree

1/2 cup water

1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

1/2 cup raisins or dried cranberries (optional)

Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

Sift Dry Ingredients:

In a medium-sized bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and allspice. Set this dry mixture aside.

Cream Butter and Sugar:

In a large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar together until the mixture is light and fluffy.

Add Eggs and Pumpkin:

Beat in the eggs one at a time, and then stir in the pumpkin puree. Mix until well combined.

Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients:

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix.

Add Water and Optional Ingredients:

Stir in the water until the batter is smooth. If desired, fold in the chopped nuts and raisins or dried cranberries.

Transfer to Pan:

Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.

Bake:

Bake in the preheated oven for about 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick or cake tester inserted into the center comes out clean. Baking times may vary, so start checking around 50 minutes.

Cool and Serve:

Allow the pumpkin bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, and then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, slice and enjoy your homemade pumpkin bread.

This pumpkin bread is perfect for breakfast, a snack, or dessert. You can also add a cream cheese frosting for an extra touch of sweetness if you like.
