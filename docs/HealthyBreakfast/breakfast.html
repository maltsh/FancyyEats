OATMEAL WITH ALMOND BUTTER AND BANANA

  • Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 ripe banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Instructions:
  • In a saucepan, combine rolled oats and almond milk.
  • Cook over medium heat until the oats are soft and the mixture thickens.
  • Top with banana slices, almond butter, and a drizzle of honey if desired.
