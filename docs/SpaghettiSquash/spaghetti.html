SPAGHETTI SQUASH

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized spaghetti squash
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings such as grated Parmesan cheese, marinara sauce, or fresh herbs

Instructions:

  • Preheat the Oven:
  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Prepare the Squash:
  • Wash the spaghetti squash and pat it dry with a clean kitchen towel.
  • Using a sharp knife, carefully cut the squash in half lengthwise. Be cautious while doing this, as spaghetti squash can be quite tough to cut through. You can also microwave the squash for a couple of minutes to soften it slightly before cutting.
  • Once cut, use a spoon to scoop out the seeds and stringy bits from the center of each half.
  • Season the Squash:
  • Drizzle a little olive oil over the inside of each squash half, and then sprinkle with salt and pepper. You can also season it with your favorite herbs or spices, such as garlic powder or Italian seasoning.
  • Roast the Squash:
  • Place the squash halves, cut side down, on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat. This helps the squash to cook evenly and develop a slightly caramelized surface.
  • Roast in the preheated oven for 35-45 minutes, or until the flesh is tender and easily pierced with a fork.
  • Scrape the Spaghetti:
  • Once the squash is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Then, take a fork and gently scrape the flesh of the squash to create long, spaghetti-like strands. The flesh will naturally separate into strands.
  • Serve:
  • Transfer the spaghetti squash strands to a serving dish. You can serve them with your choice of toppings, such as marinara sauce, grated Parmesan cheese, fresh herbs, or any other favorite pasta toppings.

Spaghetti squash is versatile and can be paired with a variety of sauces and toppings to suit your taste.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious spaghetti squash meal!
