BANANA BREAD

Ingredients:

2 to 3 ripe bananas, mashed (about 1 cup)

1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted

3/4 cup granulated sugar

1 large egg, beaten

1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

1 teaspoon baking soda

A pinch of salt

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Optional add-ins: 1/2 cup chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts or pecans) or chocolate chips

Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8-inch (10x20 cm) loaf pan.

Mash the Bananas:

In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.The riper the bananas, the better for flavor.

Add Melted Butter:

Melt the butter in a microwave or on the stovetop. Add the melted butter to the mashed bananas and stir to combine.

Mix in Sugar, Egg, and Vanilla:

Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract into the banana-butter mixture. Mix well.

Incorporate Dry Ingredients:

Sprinkle the baking soda and a pinch of salt over the banana mixture and gently stir. Add the flour and mix until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

Add Optional Ingredients:

If you're using nuts or chocolate chips, fold them into the batter at this point.

Pour into Loaf Pan:

Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.

Bake:

Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 60-70 minutes or until a toothpick or cake tester inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean.

Cool and Serve:

Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, slice and enjoy!

This banana bread is best when it's slightly warm or at room temperature.

It's a delicious treat for breakfast or as a snack.

You can also customize it by adding your

favorite mix-ins like chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruits.
