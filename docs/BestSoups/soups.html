Basic Soup Recipe:

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons cooking oil or butter

1 medium onion, diced

2-3 cloves of garlic, minced (optional)

2-3 cups of chopped vegetables (e.g., carrots, celery, bell peppers, potatoes, mushrooms, etc.)

1 cup of protein (e.g., diced chicken, ground beef, lentils, beans, or tofu)

6 cups of liquid (e.g., chicken or vegetable broth, water, or a combination)

Seasonings (e.g., salt, pepper, dried herbs, spices)

Optional additions (e.g., pasta, rice, canned tomatoes)

Fresh herbs or greens for garnish

Instructions:

Sauté Aromatics:

Heat the oil or butter in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and sauté until they become translucent.

Add Vegetables and Protein:

Add your choice of chopped vegetables and protein to the pot. Sauté them for a few minutes to develop flavor.

Pour in Liquid:

Pour in the liquid (broth, water, or a combination). You can adjust the amount of liquid to achieve your preferred consistency.

Season and Simmer:

Season the soup with salt, pepper, and any desired herbs or spices. Common options include thyme, oregano, basil, or bay leaves. Stir well, and bring the soup to a boil. Then, reduce the heat to a simmer and cover. Allow the soup to simmer until the vegetables and protein are tender.

Optional Additions:

If you want to add pasta, rice, or canned tomatoes, do so during the last 10-15 minutes of cooking, following package instructions.

Adjust Seasoning:

Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning as needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or other seasonings to taste.

Serve:

Ladle the hot soup into bowls, garnish with fresh herbs or greens, and enjoy!

Remember that this basic soup recipe is very versatile. You can adapt it to make a wide range of soups, from classic chicken noodle to hearty minestrone or creamy

tomato. Get creative with your choice of ingredients and seasonings to suit your preferences.
