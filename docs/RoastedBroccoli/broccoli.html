Air Fryer Cauliflower

ROASTED BROCCOLI
RECENT POST

Ingredients :

1 pound (about 450g) fresh broccoli florets

2-3 tablespoons olive oil

2-3 cloves garlic, minced (optional)

Salt and pepper, to taste

Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

Prepare the Broccoli:

Wash the broccoli and cut it into bite-sized florets. If the stalks are thick, you can peel and slice them into thin rounds; they are delicious when roasted as well.

Toss with Olive Oil and Seasonings:

In a large mixing bowl, toss the broccoli florets with the olive oil until they are evenly coated. You can also add minced garlic at this stage for extra flavor. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Spread on a Baking Sheet:

Spread the seasoned broccoli in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat. Make sure the broccoli pieces are not crowded; this helps them roast evenly and develop a slightly crispy texture.

Roast in the Oven:

Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast for about 20-25 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender and the edges are slightly browned. You can check for doneness by piercing a floret with a fork; it should be tender but not mushy.

Serve and Optionally Garnish:

Remove the roasted broccoli from the oven and transfer it to a serving platter. If you like, you can sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top before serving.

Roasted broccoli makes for a wonderful side dish that's both healthy and flavorful.

It pairs well with a variety of main courses, and you can customize it with additional seasonings or a squeeze of lemon juice for extra zest.
