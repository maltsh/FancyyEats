BAKED POTATO
RECENT POST

Ingredients:

4 large russet potatoes

2 tablespoons olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or your favorite melting cheese)

1/2 cup sour cream

1/4 cup chopped green onions

1/4 cup crumbled cooked bacon (optional)

1/4 cup butter

1/4 cup milk

Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

Prepare the Potatoes: Wash and scrub the potatoes to remove any dirt. Dry them with a paper towel.

Pierce the Potatoes: Use a fork to pierce each potato a few times. This allows steam to escape while baking and prevents them from bursting.

Season and Bake: Rub each potato with olive oil, and then sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place them directly on the oven rack or on a baking sheet. Bake for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until they are tender when pierced with a fork. The exact baking time will depend on the size of your potatoes.

Prepare the Filling: While the potatoes are baking, prepare the filling. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, and crumbled cooked bacon if you're using it. Set this mixture aside.

Make the Mashed Potato: In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add the milk, salt, and pepper, and stir until warm. Once the potatoes are done baking and have cooled slightly, cut off the top of each potato and scoop out the flesh into a bowl. Mash the potato flesh and gradually add the warm butter and milk mixture, mixing until you achieve the desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Stuff the Potatoes: Fill each hollowed-out potato with the cheese and sour cream mixture, making sure to stuff them generously. Place the stuffed potatoes back on the baking sheet.

Bake Again: Return the stuffed potatoes to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbling, and the tops are slightly golden.

Serve: Remove the stuffed baked potatoes from the oven, let them cool for a few minutes, and then serve hot.

Garnish: You can garnish your stuffed baked potatoes with additional chopped green onions, more grated cheese, and a dollop of sour cream if desired.

Enjoy your cheesy stuffed baked potatoes as a delicious side dish or a hearty meal!
