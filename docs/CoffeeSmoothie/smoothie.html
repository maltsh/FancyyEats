Ingredients:

1 cup brewed coffee, cooled (you can use regular or decaffeinated coffee)

1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy, such as almond milk or oat milk)

1 ripe banana, frozen or fresh

1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup (adjust to taste)

1/2 cup Greek yogurt (optional for added creaminess)

1 cup ice cubes

1-2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder (optional, for a mocha flavor)

A handful of ice cubes

Whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or coffee beans for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Brew and Cool the Coffee:

Brew a cup of coffee and allow it to cool to room temperature. You can also make coffee in advance and chill it in the refrigerator.

Blend the Ingredients:

In a blender, combine the cooled coffee, milk, ripe banana, honey or maple syrup, Greek yogurt (if using), and ice cubes. If you want a mocha flavor, add unsweetened cocoa powder.

Blend Until Smooth:

Blend all the ingredients until the mixture is smooth and creamy. If the smoothie is too thick, you can add more milk to reach your desired consistency.

Taste and Adjust:

Taste the coffee smoothie and adjust the sweetness or coffee strength to your liking. You can add more honey or coffee as needed.

Serve:

Pour the coffee smoothie into a glass. If desired, garnish with whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or a few coffee beans.

Enjoy:

Sip and enjoy your delicious coffee smoothie. It's a perfect pick-me-up for busy mornings or a refreshing afternoon treat.

Feel free to customize this coffee smoothie by adding a scoop of protein powder,

a dash of cinnamon, or even a bit of vanilla extract for extra flavor. Enjoy!
